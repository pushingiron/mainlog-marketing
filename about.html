The Story

Why I built this

I'm Jim — a pilot, aircraft owner, and software professional. I've spent years in the SaaS industry, so when I looked at the tools available for tracking my own aircraft's maintenance, I knew they could be better.

Paper logbooks get lost. Spreadsheets get unwieldy. The existing digital solutions were either too expensive, too complicated, or didn't understand how aircraft owners actually think about maintenance.

So I built Aircraft Logs. It started as a side project to track my own records, and it worked so well that I decided to open it up to other aircraft owners.

What makes Aircraft Logs different is the AI. You can literally ask it "when is my next oil change due?" in plain English, and it answers. No other maintenance tracking tool does that. I built it because I was tired of flipping through pages to find the answer myself.

Aircraft Logs is intentionally simple. One price, all features included, no upsells. I built the tool I wanted to use — and I think you'll want to use it too.
